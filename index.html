Kode Kochi Kode

Kode Kochi Kode is a 24 hours non-stop Inter-College Hackathon. It is a pilot event of Abhiyanthriki 2k19, the biennial techfest of Rajagiri School Of Engineering and Technology, Kochi, Kerala

About the event

The hackathon will have multiple tracks with specific themes, participating teams are supposed to come up with innovative solutions and make a working prototype of the same. The coding session will also involve mentors from the industry who will be providing their valuable experience and knowledge to the participants
